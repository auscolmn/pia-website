Our Methodology

How We Train

Our training programs combine three essential elements to develop genuine competency in psychedelic-assisted therapy practice.

01

Theoretical Foundation

Deep knowledge of psychedelic history, neurobiology, pharmacology, therapeutic mechanisms, research evidence, and ethical frameworks.

02

Practical Skills

Hands-on training in clinical protocols, role-plays, case demonstration, and supervised practice with experienced practitioners guiding you.

03

Integration & Reflection

Self-experiential learning, reflective practice, and personal integration work to develop the awareness and presence required for this work.

Curriculum

Topics We Cover

Our comprehensive curriculum ensures you gain mastery across all essential domains of psychedelic-assisted therapy practice.

History & Science

Psychedelic history, neurobiology, mechanisms of action

Therapeutic Techniques

Clinical protocols, preparation, dosing, integration

Harm Reduction

Safety protocols, risk assessment, crisis management

Ethics & Law

Legal framework, professional ethics, confidentiality

Specialized Modalities

MDMA, psilocybin, and ketamine-assisted therapy

Research & Evidence

Clinical trials, outcomes data, emerging research
