Psychedelics Today

Psychedelics Today is a leading educational platform dedicated to advancing scientific knowledge and public understanding of psychedelic substances, research, and their therapeutic potential.

Through our partnership with Psychedelics Today, we deliver high-quality online courses and training programs that integrate their comprehensive curriculum with our hands-on clinical expertise. This collaboration enables us to reach practitioners across Australia and internationally who are seeking credible, evidence-based education in psychedelic-assisted therapy.
