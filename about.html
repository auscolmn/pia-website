Our Story

Who We Are

A Collective of Leaders

We are a collective of clinical psychologists, psychiatrists, and specialist medical practitioners who have been actively involved in the field of psychedelic-assisted psychotherapy for many years.

Australia's recent recognition of psilocybin and MDMA as medicines by the TGA has highlighted the growing need to provide training, advice, and consultancy in the field of psychedelic-assisted therapy.

Our teachers and trainers are leaders in this field who understand the need for practitioners to be skilled at the highest level to meet this demand. We offer both theoretical and practical training for practitioners at varying levels of development and knowledge, with particular focus on the Australian context.

Our Purpose

Our Mission

Offering the Highest Quality Training & Advancing Ethical Practice

Our mission is to offer the highest quality psychedelic-assisted therapy training to clinicians in the local community, with the goal of achieving optimal treatment outcomes for those who suffer from PTSD or treatment-resistant depression.

We strive to be a leading force in the establishment of ethical and culturally responsible institutions for the use of these substances in today's society.
